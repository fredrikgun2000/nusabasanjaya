About Us

Nusa Basan Jaya

Nusa Basan Jaya was founded in 2020, which is a company engaged in the production of teak flour. Our products are used as raw material for mosquito coil, glue, plywood, wood plastic composite etc. We sell our products to fulfill both domestic and export demand. We have collaborated with several local companies to serve customers requirements from various countries. Supported by our production team that is ready to produce as much as customer needs and the existence of a quality control team, making customer satisfaction our top priority. And we can professionally handle all kinds of incoterms or commonly called International Commercial Terms.

Teak Wood Powder

Flour Teak wood or Teak wood powder is one of the main ingredients for the manufacture of mosquito coils, incense, glue mixture, mix for concrete flexibility, and is also used as material for motorcycle brake. We produce teak wood flour (teak wood powder) of sawdust teak wood (teak wood sawdust).
